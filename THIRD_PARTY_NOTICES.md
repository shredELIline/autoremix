Android resolves AndroidX, Kotlin, and Oboe through Gradle. These projects are
licensed under Apache-2.0; their copyright notices and licenses remain with
their authors. GitHub dependency review checks new dependency licenses and
high-severity vulnerabilities on pull requests.

The repository does not currently claim a reproducible dependency lock or a
generated license report. Add both before a signed release.

No third-party audio or model weights are stored in this repository.
