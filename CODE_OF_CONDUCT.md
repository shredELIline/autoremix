# Code of conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, specific, and constructive. Welcome people regardless of
identity, background, experience, or ability. Critique work, not people.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized
attention, publishing private information, and sustained disruption.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository owner's GitHub
profile. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation. Reports will be handled as confidentially
as practical.
